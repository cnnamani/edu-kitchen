Beans & plantain Recipe

Description:

this is one of the most famous local dishes in Africa and today,

we are going to be describing how to make them.

however,we have many ways and recipes to prepare them but lets keep it simple.

    Ingredients:

  • Beans
  • plantain
  • maggi
  • tomatoes and peppers
  • onions
  • Red oil

    Steps:

  1. cook the beans for 30 minutes
  2. add your blended peppers and tomatoes
  3. allow to cook for another 20 minutes
  4. add your sliced plantain and allow to cook for another 10 minu
  5. boil for 20 minutes and its ready to serve.
