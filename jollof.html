Description

this is one of the most famous local dishes in Africa and today,

we are going to be describing how to make them.

however,we have many ways and recipes to prepare them but lets keep it simple.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • meat
  • maggi
  • tomatoes
  • onions
  • vegetable oil

Steps:

  1. parboil the rice
  2. cook the meat
  3. fry the tomatoes and onions and add spiceses to it
  4. pour the parboiled rice inside the fried sauce and addd 3/4cups of water
  5. boil for 20 minutes and its ready to serve.
